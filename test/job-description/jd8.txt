
AI/ML Full-Stack Developer

We are seeking an innovative and driven AI/ML Full-Stack Developer to join our team. As an AI/ML Full-Stack Developer, you will be responsible for developing and implementing cutting-edge AI and machine learning solutions while also contributing to the full-stack development of various web applications. Your role will involve leveraging your expertise in Python, Java, and related technologies to build scalable and efficient systems. Additionally, you will play a key role in enhancing data privacy and security through techniques such as Federated Learning.

Key Responsibilities:

    Develop and deploy AI/ML models for various applications, including medical diagnosis, agriculture optimization, and IoT solutions.
    Implement Federated Learning techniques to enhance data privacy and security, particularly in sensitive domains such as healthcare.
    Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design and develop user-friendly web applications using Java EE, AngularJS, and other relevant technologies.
    Utilize deep learning frameworks like TensorFlow to create advanced AI-based systems for tasks such as plant disease diagnosis and bird species identification.
    Contribute to the full software development lifecycle, from requirements gathering to deployment and maintenance.
    Stay updated with the latest industry trends and technologies, continuously enhancing your skills and knowledge.
    Actively participate in research and publication activities, sharing insights and findings with the wider community.

Required Skills and Qualifications:

    Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
    Strong proficiency in Python, Java, and related technologies.
    Experience with AI/ML frameworks such as TensorFlow, scikit-learn, and Pandas.
    Demonstrated expertise in full-stack development, including frameworks like Flask, SpringBoot, and AngularJS.
    Solid understanding of database systems and SQL, including MySQL, SQLite, and MongoDB.
    Knowledge of web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
    Experience with version control systems like Git and developer tools like Maven.
    Excellent problem-solving skills and a passion for continuous learning.
    Strong communication and collaboration abilities, with the ability to work effectively in a team environment.

Preferred Qualifications:

    Experience with Federated Learning and other privacy-preserving machine learning techniques.
    Familiarity with IoT technologies and platforms.
    Previous research experience, including publications in reputable journals or conferences.

Benefits:

    Competitive salary and benefits package.
    Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
    Dynamic and collaborative work environment with a focus on innovation.
    Chance to work on cutting-edge AI/ML projects with real-world impact.

If you are a proactive and enthusiastic AI/ML Full-Stack Developer looking to make a meaningful contribution in a dynamic environment, we encourage you to apply and join our team!
